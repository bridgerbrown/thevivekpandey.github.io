一 stands for unity. It can denote (a) heaven or roof, (b) base (c) barrier (d) something contained
91雨 Yǔ rain Upper bar denotes the sky (a)
92天 Tiān sky/day There is man, and above him is sky (a)
93末 Mò end Originally, it is end of the tree (outermost twigs), by extension, end (a)
94旦 Dàn dawn base of the sun, start of the morning (b)
95立 Lì to stand (b)
96閂 Shuān latch (c)
97血 Xuè blood It is a vase containing blood from animal sacrifice (d)

二 is two. Number of the earth, because it makes pair with heaven. It respresents (a) two (b) two extremes
98仁 Rén (like jen) benevolence left side is man. He is linked to his neighbor by benevolence. (a)
Benevolence is fundamental virtue of confusianism
99佞Nìng eloquence, flattery left side is man, right side is woman.(a) Man is praising the woman.
100亙, 亘 Gèn extend all the way across, run all the way through A passage boat is going from one bank to another (b)
100恆 Héng Constancy, perseverance Heart crossing from beginning till the end, as the boat does (b). Left side is the heart.
有恆 = Yǒuhéng, have preseverance
永恆 = Yǒnghéng, perpetual
三 is three. (a) Number of heaven, earth and humanity.
62王 Wáng king King connects the heaven, earth and humanity
101畺 Jiāng Boundary 三 is separating two fields
102示 Shì notify Top part is 二, which is heaven. Then there is rotated 三,
which represents sun, moon and stars.
Heavens communicate to humans by permutations of sun, moon and stars
103蒜 Suàn garlic No relation with heavens or earth!
明示 = state explicitly
104丹 Dān cinnabar dash represents cinnabar in the mine, or the
crucible of the taoist alchemist with cinnabar in it.
