We will work with three images. First image is called image1.jpg, second is called image2.jpg, and last one is background.jpg.

Type 1

  • A background color
  • Border around each image
  convert \
    \( -define jpeg:size=512x576 image1.jpg -bordercolor green -border 10x10 -thumbnail 462x518 -background skyblue -gravity center -extent 512x576 \) \
    \( -define jpeg:size=512x576 image2.jpg -bordercolor green -border 10x10 -thumbnail 462x518 -background skyblue -gravity center -extent 512x576 \) \
    +append \
    output-1.jpg

Type 2

  • A background image
  • Border around each image
  convert \
    \( background.jpg -resize 1024x1024 -crop 1024x768+0+0 \) \
    \( image1.jpg -bordercolor green -border 10x10 -resize 462x518 \) -gravity west -geometry +60+0 -composite \
    \( image2.jpg -bordercolor green -border 10x10 -resize 462x518 \) -gravity east -geometry +60+0 -composite \
    output-2.jpg

Type 3

  • A background image
  • Border around each image
  • Text on each image
  convert \
    \( background.jpg -resize 1024x1024 -crop 1024x768+0+0 \) \
    \( image1.jpg -bordercolor green -border 10x10 \
                  -resize 462x518 \
                  -fill '#0008' -draw 'rectangle 25,25,170,60' \
                  -fill white   -font AvantGarde-Book  -pointsize 15 -gravity NorthWest -annotate +28+37 'Bewakoof Dress #1' \) -gravity west -geometry +60+0 -composite \
    \( image2.jpg -bordercolor green -border 10x10 \
                  -resize 462x518 \
                  -fill '#0008' -draw 'rectangle 25,25,170,60' \
                  -fill white   -font AvantGarde-Book  -pointsize 15 -gravity NorthWest -annotate +28+37 'Bewakoof Dress #2' \) -gravity east -geometry +60+0 -composite \
    output-3.jpg

Type 5

  • A background image
  • Border around each image
  • Text on each image
  • Images are tilted
  convert \
    \( background.jpg -resize 1024x1024 -crop 1024x768+0+0 \) \
    \( image1.jpg -background transparent -bordercolor green -border 10x10 \
                  -resize 462x518 \
                  -fill '#0008' -draw 'roundrectangle 12,23 180,60 10,10' \
                  -fill white -font AvantGarde-Book  -pointsize 15 -gravity NorthWest -annotate +28+37 'Bewakoof Dress #1' -rotate  10 \) -gravity west -geometry +30+0 -composite \
    \( image2.jpg -background transparent -bordercolor green -border 10x10 \
                  -resize 462x518 \
                  -fill '#0008' -draw 'roundrectangle 12,23 180,60 10,10' \
                  -fill white   -font AvantGarde-Book  -pointsize 15 -gravity NorthWest -annotate +28+37 'Bewakoof Dress #2' -rotate -15 \) -gravity east -geometry +30-40 -composite \
    output-5.jpg

Type 5

  • A background image
  • Border around each image
  • Text on each image
  • Text is tilted
    convert \
      \( background.jpg -resize 1024x1024 -crop 1024x768+0+0 \) \
      \( image1.jpg -bordercolor green -border 10x10 \
                    -resize 462x518 \

                    -fill white   -font AvantGarde-Book  -pointsize 15 -gravity NorthWest -draw "rotate -15 text 5,80 'Bewakoof Dress #1'" \) -gravity west -geometry +60+0 -composite \
      \( image2.jpg -bordercolor green -border 10x10 \
                    -resize 462x518 \
                    -fill '#0008' -draw 'rotate -15 roundrectangle -5,63 163,100 10,10' \
                    -fill white   -font AvantGarde-Book  -pointsize 15 -gravity NorthWest -draw "rotate -15 text 5,80 'Bewakoof Dress #2'" \) -gravity east -geometry +60+0 -composite \
      output-6.jpg
